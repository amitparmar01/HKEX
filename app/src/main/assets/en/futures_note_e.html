Prices on the chart are indicative only. The indicative prices are determined by:

  • if the contract has been traded up to that time on the day:
    • (when the current bid price is greater than the last recorded price), the current bid price; otherwise
    • (when the current ask price is less than the last recorded price), the current ask price; otherwise
    • where both (1)(a) and (1)(b) above do not apply, then the last recorded price; or
  • if the contract has not been traded up to that time on the day:
    • (when the current bid price is greater than the previous settlement price), the current bid price; otherwise
    • (when the current ask price is less than the previous settlement price), the current ask price; otherwise
    • where both (2)(a) and (2)(b) above do not apply, then the previous settlement price.

Data shown for the previous night's session will be removed every day at 16:30 HKT.
