1. Option buyer _______ premium while option seller ______ premium.

    1. receives; pays 
    2. pays; receives 
    The correct answer is "B"

    Explanation

    Option buyer pays premium to the seller and acquires the right to buy or sell the underlying asset at the agreed price before the option contract expires. Option seller receives the premium and grants the right to the buyer. He is in a passive position and will have to perform the agreement to buy or sell the underlying asset if so requested by the buyer before the option contract expires.

  2. By buying a call option, which of the following have you ensured?

    1. The maximum profit you will get 
    2. The actual cost of buying the underlying asset (stock/index) 
    3. The maximum cost of buying the underlying asset (stock/index) 
    The correct answer is "C"

    Explanation

    When you buy a call option, you have a right to decide whether to buy the underlying asset at pre-defined price or not. You may or may not exercise the option. The maximum cost to buy the underlying asset is the sum of the price paid to buy the option plus the strike price of the option.
