1. A call option of XYZ with expiry at Apr and strike price of $65 is selling at $0.85. The share price of XYZ is $63 now. What is the breakeven price at expiry?

    1. 63.00 
    2. 65.00 
    3. 65.85 
    The correct answer is "C"

    Explanation

    Breakeven price of call option is the sum of exercise price and premium paid.

  2. A call option of XYZ with expiry at Apr and strike price of $65 is selling at $0.85. The share price of XYZ is $63 now. What is your profit/loss if the share price at expiry is 67?

    1. 4.00 
    2. 3.15 
    3. 2.00 
    4. 1.15 
    The correct answer is "D"

    Explanation

    If share price at expiry is higher than strike price
    Profit of call option at expiry = share price - exercise price - premium paid
