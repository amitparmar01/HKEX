1. An investor has to pay margin for which of the following position(s)?

    I. Long Call

    II. Short Put (uncovered)

    III. Short Call (covered with the same quantity of underlying shares)

    IV. Short Call (uncovered)

    1. I only  
    2. II and III 
    3. II and IV 
    4. all of the above 
    The correct answer is "C"

    Explanation

    No margin is required for a long option position and a short option position that is fully covered. Only uncovered option writers have to pay margin.

  2. At HKEX, the counterparty of buyer of stock option contracts is:

    1. Unknown seller 
    2. SEHK Options Clearing House (SEOCH) 
    The correct answer is "B"

    Explanation

    At HKEX, the stock option contracts are novated so that SEOCH becomes the buyer for every seller and the seller to every buyer.
