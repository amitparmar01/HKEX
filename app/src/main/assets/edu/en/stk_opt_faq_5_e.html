• What are the trading hours for stock options?

    The trading hours of the morning session is 9:30 a.m. - 12:00 noon and the afternoon session is 1:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m.

    Note: Closing time of Stock Options for half-day trading will be 12:00 noon.

  • Is there any auction session for stock options?

    No, there is no auction session for stock options.
