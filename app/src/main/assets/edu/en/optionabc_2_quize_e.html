1. Which of the following statement(s) is/are correct?

    I. European style options can be exercised on or before the expiry date

    II. American style options can only be exercised on the expiry date

    III. SEHK's stock options are of American style

    IV. SEHK's stock options are of European style

    1. I only 
    2. III only 
    3. I, II and IV 
    4. II, III and IV 
    The correct answer is "B"

    Explanation

    SEHK's stock options are of American style and can be exercised at any time before expiry.

  2. If the underlying asset specified in a call option contract is XYZ. The call option holder can buy shares of XYZ if he exercises the option before expiry.

    1. True 
    2. False 
    The correct answer is "A"

    Explanation

    Underlying asset refers to the asset to be exchanged if the option is exercised.

  3. The current share price of XYZ is $63. If the strike price specified in a call option contract of XYZ is $65. The call option holder can buy shares of XYZ at what price if he exercises the option before expiry?

    1. 63 
    2. 65 
    The correct answer is "B"

    Explanation

    Strike price refers to the pre-determined price at which the underlying asset can be bought or sold.
