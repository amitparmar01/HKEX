1. Theoretically, the potential gain for the call option buyer is _______, while the potential gain for the call option writer is ______.

    1. limited; limited 
    2. limited; unlimited 
    3. unlimited; limited  
    4. unlimited; unlimited 
    The correct answer is "C"

    Explanation

    The potential gain for the call option buyer is theoretically unlimited as long as the underlying stock increases. But the potential gain for the call option writer is limited to the premium received.

  2. Which of the following option positions, if exercised or assigned, will result in acquiring stocks?

    I. long call

    II. long put

    III. short call

    IV. short put

    1. I and II 
    2. I and IV 
    3. II and III 
    4. II and IV 
    The correct answer is "B"

    Explanation

    The holder of a call has the right to buy stock at the strike price; the seller of a put is obliged to buy stock at the strike price if exercised.

  3. The holder of a put will realize a profit upon exercise of the option if the price of the underlying stock

    1. exceeds the exercise price 
    2. exceeds the exercise price plus the premium paid 
    3. falls below the exercise price 
    4. falls below the exercise price minus the premium paid 
    The correct answer is "D"

    Explanation

    The break-even point for the buyer of a put is the strike price of an option minus the premium paid for the option.

  4. A seller (writer) of the put has the

    1. right to sell the stock at the strike price 
    2. right to buy the stock at the strike price 
    3. obligation to buy the stock at the strike price if the put is exercised 
    4. obligation to sell the stock at the strike price if the call is exercised 
    The correct answer is "C"

    Explanation

    The option seller has received a premium for taking on an obligation of the put. If the buyer of the option exercises his right to sell the underlying stock at the strike price, the seller of the option is obliged to buy the stock at the strike price from the buyer of the option.

  5. Which of the following statement(s) is/are correct?

    I. Buyers of option contracts receive premium

    II. Sellers of option contracts are obliged to buy or sell stock

    III. Buyers of option contracts face unlimited risk

    IV. Sellers of option contracts have unlimited potential gain

    1. I only 
    2. II only 
    3. I and III 
    4. III and IV 
    The correct answer is "B"

    Explanation

    Sellers of call options are obliged to sell stocks if the options are exercised, and sellers of put options are obliged to buy stocks if the options are exercised.
